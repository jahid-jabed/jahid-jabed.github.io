About Myself

Mr. Md. Jahidul Islam has been with Chandpur Science and Technology University since April 30, 2023. Prior to his current position, he served as a Lecturer at BGMEA University of Fashion and Technology, and Prime University for more than four years.

Mr. Md. Jahidul Islam earned his bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology. He received his M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Jahangirnagar University. Mr. Md. Jahidul is currently pursuing an M.Sc. Engg. degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in order to quench his thirst for knowledge.
